Slide Show of Pics.tgu.ca

These pics are each portals onto websites. Initially they are thumbnails, but they can be increased in size and resolution. Or reset to thumb size.

Easiest on the shuffling of screen real estate is to NOT increase until all pics are selected, then browse selected, or cycle autonomously as a slideshow.

From this page you can view pictures of Gina; go to the page which holds those pics (and others); save pics to a selected list; view in a slide show; and of course there is an Easter Egg*.
